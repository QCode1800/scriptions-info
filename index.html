Privacy Policy for Scriptions

Last updated: September 18, 2025

Introduction

Scriptions ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our subscription tracking mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Subscription service names and details
  • Subscription costs and billing amounts
  • Billing cycles and renewal dates
  • Trial period information
  • Custom notes about your subscriptions

Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data for functionality purposes
  • Device identifiers for iCloud sync functionality
  • Local notification preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Help you track and manage your subscriptions
  • Sync your data across your Apple devices via iCloud
  • Send you local notifications about upcoming renewals
  • Provide app functionality and improvements

Data Storage and Sync

Local Storage

Your subscription data is primarily stored locally on your device using Core Data.

iCloud Sync

We use Apple's CloudKit service to sync your data across your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account
  • Apple's privacy policies and security measures apply to synced data
  • Only you can access your synced data through devices signed into your iCloud account

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Apple iCloud/CloudKit: For syncing your data across your devices
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (viewable within the app)
  • Delete your data (by deleting subscriptions in the app or uninstalling the app)
  • Control iCloud sync (through your device's iCloud settings)
  • Request data deletion by contacting us

Notifications

The app requests permission to send local notifications to remind you about upcoming subscription renewals. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve sending your data to external servers.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is encrypted during iCloud sync using Apple's security standards
  • Local data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We follow Apple's recommended security practices

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
